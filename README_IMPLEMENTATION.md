## 💡 Why This Plan Works

### 1. **Momentum-First Approach**
- Launch quick wins in Week 1-2 to build confidence
- Get real user feedback early
- Iterate based on data, not assumptions

### 2. **Trust Before Scale**
- Phase 1 focuses on trustworthiness (ratings, certifications, escrow)
- Phase 2 opens B2B (high-value customers)
- Phase 3 scales with proven model

### 3. **Revenue-Positive by Month 6**
- Quick wins generate organic growth
- Referral program reduces CAC
- B2B portal opens new revenue stream

### 4. **Realistic Timeline**
- 52 weeks = 1 year to full platform
- Not overly optimistic
- Includes buffer for unexpected challenges

## ⚠️ Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Slow farmer adoption | 🔴 HIGH | Early incentives, intensive marketing |
| Payment delays | 🔴 HIGH | Use Razorpay for instant settlement |
| Quality issues | 🟡 MEDIUM | Certification system, QA at pickup |
| Tech scaling | 🟡 MEDIUM | Use serverless, CDN, auto-scaling |
| Competition | 🟡 MEDIUM | First-mover advantage, high ratings |
| Regulatory | 🟡 MEDIUM | Legal review, compliance team |

### Contingency Plans

- **If slower adoption**: Extend Phase 1 by 2-4 weeks
- **If tech issues**: Use managed services (Firebase, Vercel)
- **If cash runs low**: Raise seed round at Month 6
- **If competitor appears**: Focus on quality, not price
